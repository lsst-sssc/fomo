<objective>
Deliver VIEW-05 (D-07/D-08): a single default-opt-out contact-visibility checkbox on the public
submission form that, when set, exposes a run's `contact_person`/`contact_email` to anonymous
visitors on the per-campaign table — while preserving the existing "restrict the queryset, not just
the template" PII discipline (15-RESEARCH.md Pitfall 1).

Purpose: Let submitters choose public attribution without weakening the codebase's SQL-level PII
gate. Opted-out rows must stay physically absent from the non-staff SELECT, exactly as today.

Output: A new `contact_public_opt_in` `BooleanField` (+ additive migration), a mirrored form
checkbox persisted through `form_valid`, and a `Case`/`When` queryset-level per-row PII gate.

This plan is structurally independent of the site-disambiguation plans (21-01/03/04); it shares only
the `campaign_views.py` file, and its edits are confined to `CampaignRunTableView` and
`CampaignRunSubmissionView.form_valid` (never the approval-queue/decision views those plans touch).
</objective>
