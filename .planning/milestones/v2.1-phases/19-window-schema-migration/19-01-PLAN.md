<objective>
Replace CampaignRun's single-night obs_date/ut_start/ut_end representation with a
nullable window_start/window_end DateField pair, and close the TBD-row duplicate gap
with two partial UniqueConstraints — shipped as one combined, non-reversible migration
that backfills existing rows and removes leftover duplicate fixture rows first.

Purpose: This is the schema foundation the entire phase depends on. No consumer plan
(coverage-gap, display/forms, CSV import) can compile against the new fields until this
migration and model change land. Implements D-01 (hard cutover), D-02 (single combined
migration), D-07 (delete known duplicates), D-08 (generic re-runnable dedup with logging).
Output: the new model shape, migration 0004, and the model-level tests proving SCHED-02/03/04.
</objective>
