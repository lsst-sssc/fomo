uncertain-scheduling spike and capture real, redacted evidence into the Findings
sections of `18-DECISION.md`. This resolves the four empirical questions Plan 02's
recommendations depend on: what the real `Obs. Date`/`UT Time Range` cell shapes

`18-DECISION.md` is committed from this plan.

recommendations in real live-test evidence rather than documentation guesswork —
the same discipline Phase 13 used against the real ESO API.

throwaway `fuzzy_match_probe.py` (uncommitted).
