<objective>
Build the shared, anonymous, throttled HTMX live-search endpoint that both entry surfaces
(Plan 02's public form and approval-queue widget; Plan 03's sites-needing-review row) will
call. This plan delivers the endpoint end-to-end but wires no widget yet — it is the
foundation the two consumer plans depend on.

Purpose: Close the Phase 21 gap where fuzzy matching only ran once against the
originally-submitted `site_raw`. A live endpoint lets a submitter/staff member search for a
site by typing something *different* from the original text, substring-first (so `faulkes`
surfaces both Faulkes telescopes — the operator's motivating example that plain difflib
demonstrably cannot do).

Output: A new `substring_or_fuzzy_match_candidates()` matcher + a zero-dependency per-IP
throttle in `campaign_utils.py`; a GET-only, anonymous `SiteSearchView` returning a rendered
HTML fragment; a `campaigns:site_search` URL; a new `campaigns/partials/site_search_results.html`
partial; and a new `test_campaign_site_search.py` test module.
