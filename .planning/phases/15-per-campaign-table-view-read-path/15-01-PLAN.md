<objective>
Build the per-campaign table read path: a sortable, paginated, filterable `django-tables2` +
`django-filter` view listing every `CampaignRun` for a campaign (`TargetList`), plus a plain
campaigns list page — both open to anonymous visitors, with contact PII gated to staff at the
queryset layer.

Purpose: Replace the shared 3I/ATLAS coordination spreadsheet with a first-class FOMO table
(VIEW-01), filterable by lifecycle status and collaboration flag (VIEW-04), never leaking contact
PII to anonymous viewers (VIEW-03).
Output: `campaign_tables.py`, `campaign_filters.py`, `campaign_views.py`, `campaign_urls.py`,
two templates, urls wiring, and a `test_campaign_views.py` module proving VIEW-01/03/04.
</objective>
